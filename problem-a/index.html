Bojji

Bojji is a character from the anime Ranking of Kings. He is a teenage boy who has an abnormally small body. He became the King of Bosse Kingdom after his dad, a respected king, died, but no one takes him seriously because he's so small.

3 reasons why I love Bojji

  • He's a humble King. He doesn't think of himself as any better than other people.
  • He has a fire outfit. Crown, green cape, blue shirt.
  • He's brave even though he is easily overpowered by others.
